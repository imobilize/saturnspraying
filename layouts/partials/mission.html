Crafting Premier Metal Fabrications Since 1963

Thames Wire Productions, established in 1963, has steadfastly delivered top-tier metal fabrications for both the film and construction industries. Our expertise encompasses stainless steel balustrades, aluminum, brass, and various metals, in addition to mild steel. Notably, we have supplied balustrades to renowned global retailers and multinational corporate headquarters.

With a commitment to precision and client satisfaction, all our equipment is housed in-house, including tube rolling, bending, sheet forming, rolling, and advanced machine shop facilities for turning and milling. Experience excellence with Thames Wire Productions.

What truly sets Thames Wire Productions apart is their commitment to maintaining in-house capabilities. With cutting-edge facilities encompassing tube rolling, bending, sheet forming and rolling, as well as a fully-equipped machine shop for turning and milling, they exercise precise control over the entire production process. This dedication to craftsmanship and precision extends to their client-centric approach, where they collaborate closely with clients to transform their unique designs into tangible realities. In essence, Thames Wire Productions stands as a paragon of excellence in the metal fabrication industry, fortified by a rich legacy, a prestigious clientele, and an unwavering commitment to delivering exceptional services tailored to the highest standards of quality and precision.
